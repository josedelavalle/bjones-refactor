  • Meet the owner
    • Irene is a graduate of Artistic Academy and The Institute for Therapeutic Massage and is the owner of b.jones. It is her philosophy that organic products, organic cleaning methods, and offering organic teas/snacks is a client right. Irene believes in treating skin conditions as they appear and enjoys giving body spa treatments and relaxing facials. A Morristown resident since 1999, Irene is a member of Assumption Church and parent volunteer for Assumption School, and has volunteered with several other local organizations. She currently serves on the board for Morristown Women in Business and is an assistant pod leader for the Morristown Pod for Believe Inspire Grow. Irene's mission is to give back to the community one face at a time.

  • Spa Policies & Etiquette

    • Sanitation:

      b.Jones uses organic cleaning supplies that are better for you & the environment.

    • Appointments:

      Please arrive 15 minutes to your appointment time. If you are late for a service, you may be cut short. A major credit card is required to reserve your appointment. For your convenience, we accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, & Discover.

    • Cancellations:

      24 hour notice is required to cancel your appointment. If you cancel your appointment in less than 24 hours, your credit card will be charged 50% of your service.

    • Satisfaction Guarantee:

      All retail returns will be refunded within 7 days of purchase. If you are unhappy with a service, please let us know immediately.

    • Children:

      Children are not permitted in the spa unless they are here for an event, service, or invited to the spa.

    • Gratuities:

      Gratuity may be given directly to your therapist or added to the bill.
